The Story of Straw Hat in the first half of Grand Line

Straw Hat Pirates is a group of pirate that led by a 17 years old Monkey D. Luffy. He started his journey at his home town in East Blue by getting crew. Crew Member gathering was very tough in East Blue, Luffy Gathered Zoro, Nami, Usopp, and Sanji

After a long journey by fighting many antagonist, The Straw Hat Pirates entered Grand Line, the legendary sea that has many mystery, and accidently recruited Vivi as their new Crew. Sadly Vivi need to stay at his kingdom Arabasta to continue his role as a queen. But then Robin offered herself to become their crew which previously she was a minor antagonist in Arabasta.

After then Luffy and his crew went to sky island, beating God Enel, and experienced a great trip there. Then the Straw Hats went to Water Seven and recruited Franky and got a new ship called Thousand Sunny Go. After that they accidently went to Florian Triangle and recruited Brook as their new Crew

These are the montages that went really cool and many fans admired it

The Journey of Straw Hat Pirates still continue, until their entire crew achieved what their dreaming of
